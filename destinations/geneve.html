Where the World
Convenes.

Geneva is a city of quiet power. Home to the United Nations, the Red Cross, and the world's most prestigious watchmakers, it blends Swiss precision with international sophistication. The iconic Jet d'Eau rises 140 metres above Lake Geneva, a beacon visible from every approach.

Costa Driver connects the French Riviera to Geneva with the same standard of excellence our clients expect. Whether arriving for business at the Palais des Nations, shopping on Rue du Rhone, or continuing to Alpine resorts, we ensure every transition is flawless.
