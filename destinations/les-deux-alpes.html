The Alpine Pinnacle

At 3,600 meters, Les Deux-Alpes stands as Europe's highest ski resort. Beyond the slopes, it's a sanctuary for those who seek mountain refinement—summer hiking, private residences of extraordinary altitude, and experiences accessible only to those with the right connections.

Costa Driver has been coordinating arrivals and departures on these mountains for over a decade. We understand Alpine logistics, Michelin restaurants at elevation, and the precise timing required for seamless mountain experiences.

Winter Season Transfers

December through April: precision transfers from Geneva (1 hour) and Grenoble (1.5 hours) airports. We monitor weather, road conditions, and flight times to ensure flawless timing.

Summer Mountain Access

June through September: hiking expeditions, private lodge coordination, and scenic transfers through the Hautes-Alpes. We know every mountain pass and private residence.

Group Logistics

Our V-Class accommodates 4-8 passengers with luggage space for multiple days. We coordinate group timing, special equipment transport, and multi-day Alpine itineraries.

Concierge Coordination

Michelin restaurant bookings at altitude, helicopter access for remote lodges, and coordination with private Alpine residences. Mountains made seamless.

Getting Here

  • From Geneva: 60-90 min depending on season and road conditions
  • From Grenoble: 90-120 min via Bourg-d'Oisans
  • From Lyon: 2.5-3 hours

Best Times to Visit

  • Winter (Dec-Apr): Ski season, perfect snow, Alpine dining
  • Summer (Jun-Sep): Hiking, mountain biking, clear skies
  • Shoulder (May, Oct): Fewer crowds, lower altitude options
